  • must own a computer.

Description:

Technoligical Creations introduce children to programming using Scratch, a visual block-based programming language developed by MIT. Scratch is a fun and interactive way for kids to learn coding by creating their own games, animations, and stories. The roadmap is structured to progressively build skills, starting from the basics and moving towards more advanced projects.

After the diploma, you will work on 3 graduation projects: A sprite moving through a simple maze - A basic animated story with moving characters - A simple musical project with sound effects, and this improves your chances in the market because you add them to your CV.

We provide you with the tickets system, through which it can be uploaded to the system if you encounter any problem during the diploma, and the Instructor will answer you or enter with you in a free private session in order to ensure that you have achieved the greatest possible benefit during the diploma.
