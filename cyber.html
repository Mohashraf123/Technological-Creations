Description:

technological-creations introduce you the most powerful Cybersecurity diploma accredited by CISCO Academy, with the strongest content.

Studying during the diploma:

  • Network Essentials
  • Network Security
  • Cyber Ops
  • Qrader and Splunk

Learn the basic concepts of networks; different network types, how they are connected and the different layers that make up a network

Learn how to secure the network. You will study the techniques that are used to secure the network including firewalls, IPS, IDS and Access Control

Learn the attacks that can happen on the network. Examine closely how the attacks are perceived on the system. Learn to recognize the attacks from different system logs.

You will gain an excellent knowledge of the most common malwares that are used today by cyber criminals. By understanding the attacks, you will learn how best to defend against them.

You will learn the how to use one of the most commonly used tool in the industry Q-Rader and understand how the SIEM solution work and its architecture.

Through multiple workshops, you will be trained on tools used in the industry today and with that you can collect your rewards and certificate for mastering those challenges.

After the diploma, you will be able to receive multiple certificates from CISCO as you access their cite and participate in the challenges.

We provide you with the tickets system, through which it can be uploaded to the system if you encounter any problem during the diploma, and the Instructor will answer you or enter with you in a free private session in order to ensure that you have achieved the greatest possible benefit during the diploma.
