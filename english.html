Curriculum of this diploma

Listening

  • Listen to basic phrases, greetings, and simple dialogues.
  • Practice with beginner-level podcasts or videos with subtitles.

Speaking

  • Practice introducing yourself and exchanging simple information.
  • Learn common phrases for everyday situations (e.g., shopping, asking for directions).

Reading

  • Read simple texts like short stories, dialogues, or children's books.
  • Focus on understanding the gist and basic vocabulary.

Grammar

  • Focus on basic grammar structures (present simple, present continuous, basic prepositions).
  • Learn sentence formation, articles (a, an, the), and pronouns.

Requirements

  • Having a background in at least one programming language.
  • Student or graduate of the Faculty of Engineering or Faculty of Computer, Artificial intelligence and Information Sciences.
  • must own a computer.

Technoligical Creations introduces learning English, from beginner to advanced levels. Each stage focuses on different aspects of the language, including listening, speaking, reading, writing, vocabulary, and grammar.
